Welcome to Green Hub International Schools Kaduna (GHIS) was established in 2020. Located at KM 12, Kaduna-Abuja Express Way, Kaduna, Nigeria BUT now located on Stephen Rijoh Shekari Drive formerly Gwari Avenue, Barnawa Kaduna. GHIS has a quiet and serene environment, conducive for learning, GREEN HUB INTERNATIONAL SCHOOLS (GHIS) KADUNA provides the best in terms of academic, moral and spiritual guidance for students Over the years, we have been acknowledged for maintaining standard in terms of service delivery. Our mission has been to develop children that are harbingers of Christ in character, culture and craftsmanship

Mission

To Produce Excellent, God Fearing Students Without Discriminating, Using Sound Christian Principles Inline With Government Regulations on Education at an Affordable Cost for the Benefit of Society

Vission

To be among the Top 5 Secondary schools in Kaduna State and among the Best 20 Secondary Schools in Nigeria by 2030
